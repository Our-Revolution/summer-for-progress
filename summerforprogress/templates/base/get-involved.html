The Summer for Progress: Let’s Move Our Country Forward

From sharing social media posts to hosting town-halls and lobbying meetings, there’s a way for everyone to get involved.

1. Join us on Slack

After you’ve signed the petition, join us on Slack! Slack is a messaging app for teams, and it’s what we use to stay connected with folks all around the country.

Organizers from national partner organizations, as well as other activists and hosts, are waiting for you! We’ll be there to answer questions or walk you through key steps of the action, or maybe you can lend your experience someone else. We’re in this together!
